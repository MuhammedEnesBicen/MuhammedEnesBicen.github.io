Privacy Policy

Vocab: Vocabulary Feed

Last Updated: 2025 December

1. No Personal Data Collection

Vocab does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. The app operates entirely on-device and does not require any form of account, login, or personal information.

2. App Functionality

The application’s sole purpose is to display vocabulary content in a feed format and provide optional pronunciation playback. All data used within the app (such as words, meanings, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on the user’s device and never transmitted to any external server.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob for showing advertisements. These services may collect certain data as described in their own privacy policies. You can review Google’s policies on their official website.

4. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since Vocab collects no personal information, it is safe for all age groups.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the new version.
